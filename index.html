Welcome to My Portfolio

I have created this website using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The purpose for doing this is to show the skills that I have learned during my process of learning how to code. This website was created during the beginning stages of my learning and will continually change as I learn new tricks that make coding easier. The contents of this site will display a multitude of things, including my reasons for learning, how I learned to code, why it interests me, and the issues that I have come across in my journey.

What Peaked My Interest?

I am not entirely sure what actually made me want to start coding or even think that it was something that I could do. During my undergrad, I took a basic Comtuter Science course as an elective. A few of the projects were based around writing a Javascript code, a C++ code and another that I do not remember. I found these to be very confusing and did not have a lot of guidance. Unfortunately, this turned me off to the idea of learning code.

It wasnt until a couple years after I graduated that I hit a slump and didnt know what I wanted to do for a career. I started to research coding languages and wanted to learn something that I could play with. I discovered HTML. It started to make sense to me as I looked into it more. I spent a few years palying with some of it but didnt spend a lot of time learning. It wasnt until I got a job working for a company that wanted me to start learning a little JavaScript.

The company knew that I did not know JS but they wanted me to learn basics to help my supervisor. I did not tell them I knew coding. There was a lot more to the job. After working there for a little over a year, I made a desicion to go back to school and get a Master's degree. It is at this point that I am starting this website.
